# Residual Analysis

## Method

Predicted sales were calculated using the selected multiple regression model.

Residual

Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales

---

## Largest Positive Residuals

These stores performed significantly better than predicted.

Possible reasons:

- Strong local demand
- Exceptional management
- Successful local promotions
- Loyal customer base
- Favorable competition

---

## Largest Negative Residuals

These stores performed below model expectations.

Possible reasons:

- Operational issues
- Local competition
- Stock shortages
- Staffing problems
- Temporary market conditions

---

## Business Interpretation

Large positive residuals indicate stores outperforming expectations.

Large negative residuals indicate opportunities for operational improvement.

Management should investigate both groups to identify best practices and operational challenges.

---

## Overall Assessment

Residuals appear randomly distributed, suggesting the regression model captures much of the variation while acknowledging unexplained business factors.
