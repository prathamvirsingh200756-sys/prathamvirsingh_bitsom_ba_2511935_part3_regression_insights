# Model Comparison

## Model 1

Name

Simple Regression – Marketing Spend

Dependent Variable

Monthly Sales

Independent Variable

Marketing Spend

Business Interpretation

Stores investing more in marketing generally achieve higher monthly sales.

Strengths

Easy to interpret.

Weaknesses

Ignores operational factors.

---

## Model 2

Name

Simple Regression – Footfall

Dependent Variable

Monthly Sales

Independent Variable

Footfall

Business Interpretation

Higher customer traffic is associated with higher sales.

Strengths

Useful operational KPI.

Weaknesses

Does not account for conversion efficiency.

---

## Model 3

Name

Multiple Linear Regression

Dependent Variable

Monthly Sales

Independent Variables

- Marketing Spend
- Footfall
- Inventory Availability
- Customer Rating
- Store Type Dummy

Business Interpretation

This model explains monthly sales using multiple business drivers simultaneously.

Advantages

- Better prediction
- More realistic
- Supports management decisions
- Controls for multiple variables

Limitations

- Association is not causation
- May omit external business factors
- Sensitive to multicollinearity

---

# Overall Comparison

The multiple regression model provides the strongest business insights because it evaluates multiple operational variables together instead of one variable at a time.
